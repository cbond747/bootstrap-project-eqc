About Us

Founded in 2021, Sweetafy is a beloved local cafe that brings joy and sweetness to our community. Our journey began with a simple idea: to offer unique, modern and vibrant desserts and beverages in a cozy, welcoming environment.

Our Offerings

We offer a wide variety of delicious desserts and beverages, including our signature gelato, crepes, bubble waffles, and a selection of cold and hot drinks.
